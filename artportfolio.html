Bee Happy: Bee Pollination Collages

These collages include pictures of Bees pollinating, taken by myself. Plastic LionFish

These images show a Lionfish sculpture that was created for the purpose of a project which highlighted the horrible outcomes of plastic pollution in the ocean. This project was to bring awareness and prevent as much pollution as possible by creating sea creatures completely from plastic that were endangered. The Lionfish is not yet endangered but they are a great cause of other tropical fishes being endangered. Due to Lionfish not being as edible as other fishes for Humans and other animals in the oceanic ecosystem, they lack predators and reproduce over hundreds of thousands of eggs in the span of a year. Lionfish will eventually become extinct as their population is growing but their food source is depleting so sadly they will be the end of their own demise.

Rana de Los Tainos

These are images of a sculpture which is supposed to capture the essence of culture. This frog sculpture was carved carefully for the span of a few days, each crevice carefully thought and mapped out on paper before physically enacted. This frog sculpture is supposed to have an old Taino feel. (Taino's are the native people of Puerto Rico, these were the first native people to the island before colonization and the slave trade.)

Why Do I Create Art?

I have always been interested in arts and crafts since I was a child as both my parents partook in being creative. Since I was in middle school I have always continued to practice and better my skills in order to pursue art as a side hobby. I enjoy making all types of art and using different types of mediums ranging from collages and sculptures to wires and bracelet making. Art is a great expressive outlet for when I am stressed and it is always comforting to engage in, even when I have artist block or am frustrated with a piece, I still enjoy and ind value in art. I did not follow through with art as a main career path because there are alot of people going into the art field and there is not a garuntee you will be chosen to do something great, especially because when entering a career like art is is way better to have connections. Unfortunately, I don't want to risk my financial stability and happiness to pursue art but it will always hold a special place in my heart.
