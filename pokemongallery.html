Pokémon Gallery

1. Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon. Pikachu is known for its cute appearance and its ability to generate electricity. Electric type Pokemon are very effective against Flying and Water-type Pokemon and weak against Ground-type Pokemon.

2. Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon. Charmander is recognized for its flame-tipped tail and its fiery spirit. Fire type Pokemon are very effective against Grass, Bug, Ice, and Steel-type Pokemon and are weaker against Water, Rock, and Ground-type Pokemon.

3. Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass and Poison-type Pokémon and is the first Pokémon in the Pokédex. Bulbasaur is known for his teals color and spouting plant on its back. Grass-type Pokemon are effective against Water, Ground, and Rock-type Pokemon while being weaker to Fire, Bug, Flying, and Poison-type Pokemon. Since Bulbasaur is also a Poison-type Pokemon this Pokemon is strong against Water, Ground, Rock, and Fairy-type Pokemon, while being weaker to Fire, Flying, Psychic, Bug, and Ice-type Pokemon.

4. Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon. Squirtle is known for its playful nature and ability to shoot water from its mouth. Water-type Pokémon are super effective against Fire, Ground, and Rock-type Pokemon while being weaker to Electric and Grass-type Pokemon.

5. Jigglypuff

Jigglypuff is a Normal and Fairy-type Pokémon. Jigglypuff is known for its ability to sing lullabies that make others fall asleep. Since Jigglypuff is both a Normal and fairy-type Pokemon, Jigglypuff is super effective against Fighting, Dragon, and Dark-type Pokemon while weaker towards Steels, Poison, and Fighting-type Pokemon

End of Gallery
